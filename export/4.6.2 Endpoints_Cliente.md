# 4.6.2 Endpoints Cliente

> Endpoints para clientes autenticados (B2C, B2B, Corporate+).

---

## Endpoints Citas

| Método | Ruta | Descripción |
|--------|------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/citas` | Listar mis citas |
| POST | `/api/citas` | Crear nueva cita |
| GET | `/api/citas/:id` | Ver detalle cita |
| PATCH | `/api/citas/:id` | Modificar cita |
| DELETE | `/api/citas/:id` | Cancelar cita |

---

## Endpoints Vehículos

| Método | Ruta | Descripción |
|--------|------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/vehiculos` | Mis vehículos |
| POST | `/api/vehiculos` | Agregar vehículo |
| PATCH | `/api/vehiculos/:id` | Modificar |
| DELETE | `/api/vehiculos/:id` | Eliminar |

---

## Endpoints Pagos

| Método | Ruta | Descripción |
|--------|------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/pagos` | Historial pagos |
| POST | `/api/pagos/checkout` | Crear checkout |
| GET | `/api/facturas` | Mis facturas |
