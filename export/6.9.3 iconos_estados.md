# 6.9.3 Iconos Estados

> Iconos para estados y feedback.

---

## Catálogo

| Icono | Nombre | Iconify | Color |
|-------|--------|---------|-------|
| ✓ | success | `i-heroicons-check-circle` | `#22C55E` |
| ✕ | error | `i-heroicons-x-circle` | `#EF4444` |
| ⚠ | warning | `i-heroicons-exclamation-triangle` | `#F59E0B` |
| ℹ | info | `i-heroicons-information-circle` | `#3B82F6` |

---

## Implementación @nuxt/icon

```vue
<!-- Con colores semánticos -->
<Icon name="i-heroicons-check-circle" class="text-green-500" />
<Icon name="i-heroicons-x-circle" class="text-red-500" />
<Icon name="i-heroicons-exclamation-triangle" class="text-yellow-500" />
<Icon name="i-heroicons-information-circle" class="text-blue-500" />

<!-- En alerts -->
<UAlert icon="i-heroicons-check-circle" color="success" title="Guardado" />
```
